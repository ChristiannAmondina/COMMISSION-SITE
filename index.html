    • ABOUT THE WEBSITE

    "Hello, I am Parkchanq, the author of this website. Thank you for visiting! This platform allows me to freely share my works and other content with my followers. Feel free to navigate and explore the various sections. The website is designed to be versatile, so please take your time to explore. If you follow me on any of my social media accounts, you already have an idea of the theme and concept of my art. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. Don't forget to contact us through the 'Contact' section if you encounter any problems or bugs, as the website is still undergoing updates and development."

    I have been engaged in art and drawing since childhood, developing an innate interest in it even before kindergarten. Over the years, I've consistently showcased and shared my artistic abilities, welcoming constructive criticism as a means to grow. Now as an adult, reflecting on those early years, my artistic journey has led me through various aspects of the art world. I experimented with different mediums and concepts, finding temporary joy in creating illustrations until I discovered Surrealism.

    Surrealism has proven to be transformative for me, aiding in managing my emotions and expressions more effectively. It serves as a form of art that touches my soul, providing an escape from the fast-changing world. The resonance people have with my Surrealist creations is truly amazing and serves as a primary motivation for continuing this artistic pursuit.

    Beyond Surrealism, I also have a passion for creating concept art, illustrations, and character designs. Exploring these diverse aspects of art brings me immense joy, as I firmly believe that art is versatile‚Äîevery action, every moment we live and breathe is a form of artistic expression.

    Growing up as an artist, I used to create art on my phone, using my thumb, whether sitting in a simple chair or at a desk wherever I went. It may seem like a challenging journey for a digital artist, but I never let it become a barrier. Instead, I viewed it as a stepping stone, understanding that each step is a contribution to my growth. Reflecting on the past, I initially believed such details didn't matter much, but they have shaped me into the person I am today.

    Considering the words I've shared, it's evident that I am still on the path to becoming the artist I aspire to be. However, I also recognize the privilege of being far from where I started. Every step and progress I make holds significance for me. My desire is to share my creations with the world and let my hands tell a story. Ultimately, I aspire to evolve into a great illustrator, inspiring others and sharing meaningful ideas.

    Apart from pursuing art, I am also a computer tech student enrolled in a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program. In this course, our primary focus is on programming and addressing real-world challenges in the rapidly evolving technology landscape. Despite being in the field of computer science, our emphasis lies not in developing websites and software, as is the case for information technology students.

    The website you are currently viewing was designed by me, as I took a keen interest in creating a platform for showcasing my art. I dedicated time to researching and studying how to build a website, and after a challenging five-month process, this is the result. Although creating the website posed significant challenges, I take pride in this accomplishment. It has been a valuable learning experience, expanding my knowledge beyond my artistic passion.

    Even though art and programming may not seem directly connected in dictionaries, I believe that everything is linked by its unique strings. I had always harbored a desire to own a website crafted solely through programming languages, and now that I have achieved it, I am immensely grateful to the individuals who supported me throughout this journey. Their belief in me served as the motivation to persist in creating things I am passionate about.
